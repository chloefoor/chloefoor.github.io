Honors and Awards

Letters and Science Honors Program

A route provided by the College of Letters and Science that offers small, Honors-level courses for students who want to go above and beyond the typical L&S experience at UW.

Dean's List: Fall 2020, Spring 2021, Fall 2021, Spring 2022

Requirements: 12+ credits taken with a semsester GPA of at least 3.850

Thomas W. Parker Scholarship: Fall 2021

A scholarship given to a continuing student majoring in the humanities or social sciences

William F. Allen Award: Spring 2022 for essay 'The Alleged Atheism of Christopher Marlowe'

One of three awards given out to the best historial essays of term-paper size and scope. Paper must be at least 10 pages and have been written for a history course.
